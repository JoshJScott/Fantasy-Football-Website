The League


League Format

  1. Buy-in and Payout

    1. Everyone can buy-in for whatever amount they want.

    2. The payout will be proportional to the amount you put in, but no advantage is given to higher buy-ins. For a full explanation, click here

    3. The highest bidder will immediately be refunded the amount that he paid in over the next highest bidder

    4. Current Payout Settings:

      1. League Finish:

1st place : 35%

2nd place : 20%

3rd place : 10%

      1. Regular Season Points:

1st place : 20%

2nd place : 10%

      1. Most points against:

1st place : 5%


  1. Teams

    1. The league can have up to 32 teams (must be an even number) – the composition of the teams can be altered as the league gets bigger or smaller

    2. Additions: New additions must take over an existing team if anyone has quit – otherwise, they will be added normally with no keepers


  1. Playoffs

    1. There will be an 8 team, 3 week playoff. Weeks 15-17

    2. Rather than a traditional bracket playoff, higher seeded teams will always play lower seeded teams in the playoffs. For an ex. click here.

    3. The toilet bowl is the consolation bracket. The losing 8 teams will have to battle to not get last place



  1. Platform – app

    1. League uses Sleeper

    2. ESPN is a solid secondary app to have in for “expert picks” and a more professionally vetted pre-draft ADP


League Settings


  1. Team Composition

    1. Default composition except for:

    2. If more than 12 or more teams are in the league, the TE slot will be removed, leaving 2RB, WR, and 2 Flex

    3. If more than 16 teams are in the league, the QB slot will be replaced by a super flex slot (NOT a 2 QB league – this gives the option to play anyone in your QB slot). Click here for an example


  1. Scoring

    1. Mostly default scoring except:

    2. Bonusses (bonusses stack):

      1. QBs:

1 point for 40+ yd pass

1 point for 40+ yd TD

1 point for 50+ yd TD

      1. Rushing and Receiving:

3 point for 40+ yd

1 point for 40+ yd TD

1 point for 50+ yd TD

      1. K & Def:

Extra penalty for kicks missed under 30 yds

15 point bonus for shutout

    1. If the league is big enough to remove the TE spot, TEs will be given an extra PPR (double PPR for TEs)


  1. Week-to-Week Match Ups

    1. League Median Matchups: Each week you are playing your opponent AND the team median

    2. No conferences or divisions


  1. Waivers

    1. Waivers will be by FAAB budget. Explanation here

    2. Be strategic and don’t waste your entire budget week 1.





  1. Trades

    1. Trades happen fast – only 1 day to veto

    2. You can trade future picks. For more info, click here





  1. Keeper Settings

    1. There will be 2 keepers per year. You do not have to use them.

    2. The keeper will cost you 1 round from the previous year. For ex. players drafted Round 14 can be kept and take the place of your Round 13th pick. If kept another year, they would take the place of your 12th round pick.

    3. Thus, no one picked in round 1 can be kept, and anyone from round 2 can only be kept for a single year.



    1. Players that were traded can be kept – using their original draft location as cost.



    1. Players can be kept for a maximum of 3 years (one draft year and 2 keeper years).


    1. If a kept player is traded to you, the 3 years does not reset. Think of it like a player’s contract.
